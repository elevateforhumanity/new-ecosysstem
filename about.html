About Elevate for Humanity

Empowering individuals through workforce development, career training, and comprehensive support services. Serving our community since 2018.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive workforce development and career training programs that empower individuals to achieve economic self-sufficiency and career advancement through hands-on learning, wraparound services, and community partnerships.

We specialize in serving veterans, individuals with employment barriers, and underemployed adults who are ready to transform their careers and lives through quality training and support.

Our Vision

To be the leading workforce development organization in Indiana, recognized for our innovative training programs, exceptional outcomes, and commitment to serving those who need it most.

We envision a community where every individual has access to quality career training and the support needed to achieve their professional goals and contribute to economic growth.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Elevate for Humanity emerged from a recognized need for comprehensive workforce development services in Indianapolis. Our founders, with backgrounds in education, workforce development, and veteran services, saw an opportunity to create an organization that would truly serve those facing employment barriers.

Starting with a small facility and a handful of programs, we quickly gained recognition for our innovative approach to workforce development. Our emphasis on hands-on learning, wraparound services, and real-world job placement set us apart from traditional training providers.

In 2019, we achieved WIOA Eligible Training Provider status, allowing us to serve federally-funded students. This milestone enabled us to expand our programs and serve more individuals in need of career training and support.

Today, we operate multiple training programs across healthcare, technology, and skilled trades, with specialized services for veterans and individuals with employment barriers. Our success is measured not just in job placements, but in the transformed lives of our graduates.
