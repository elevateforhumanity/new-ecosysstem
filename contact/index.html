Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for programs?

Visit our Programs page to learn about available training opportunities and application requirements.

Are programs free?

Many of our programs are available at no cost to eligible participants through WIOA funding and other workforce development initiatives.

What are the requirements?

Requirements vary by program. Generally, participants must be eligible to work in the US and meet specific program criteria.

How long are the programs?

Program length varies from short-term certifications (2-4 weeks) to comprehensive training programs (3-6 months).
