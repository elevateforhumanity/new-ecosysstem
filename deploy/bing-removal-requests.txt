BING WEBMASTER TOOLS REMOVAL REQUESTS
====================================

Go to: https://www.bing.com/webmasters/

1. Select your site: elevateforhumanity.org
2. Click "Block URLs" in left menu
3. Enter URLs to block:

URLs to block:
- https://elevateforhumanity.org/
- https://www.elevateforhumanity.org/
- https://elevateforhumanity.org/*

4. Set block type: "Block page and remove from search results"
5. Click "Submit"

ALTERNATIVE: Remove entire site
- Go to "Configure My Site" 
- Click "Delete Site"
- Confirm deletion
