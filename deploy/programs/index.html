Workforce Development Programs

Elevate for Humanity offers INTraining-approved workforce development programs designed to connect participants with meaningful employment opportunities.

CPR/OSHA Certification

Essential safety certifications required for many workplace environments. Includes hands-on training and certification testing.

  • CPR/AED Certification
  • OSHA 10-Hour Safety Training
  • First Aid Certification
  • Workplace Safety Protocols

MOS Certifications

Microsoft Office Specialist certifications to demonstrate proficiency in essential workplace software.

  • Microsoft Word Specialist
  • Microsoft Excel Specialist
  • Microsoft PowerPoint Specialist
  • Microsoft Outlook Specialist

ServSafe Certification

Food safety certification for careers in the hospitality and food service industry.

  • Food Safety Fundamentals
  • HACCP Principles
  • Allergen Management
  • Manager Certification

Beauty Career Educator

Comprehensive training for careers in the beauty and cosmetology industry.

  • Cosmetology Fundamentals
  • Hair Styling Techniques
  • Skin Care Basics
  • Business Skills for Beauty Professionals

Apprenticeship Programs

Hands-on apprenticeships with local employer partners across multiple industries.

  • Construction Trades
  • Healthcare Support
  • Manufacturing
  • Information Technology

WIOA Eligible Programs

Programs eligible for Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act funding for qualified participants.

  • No-cost training for eligible participants
  • Career counseling and support
  • Job placement assistance
  • Ongoing career development
