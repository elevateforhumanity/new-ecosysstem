Important: This policy applies to all paid training programs. Federally-funded programs (WIOA, etc.) may have different cancellation procedures.

Cancellation Before Program Start

Students may cancel enrollment and receive a full refund if:

  • Cancellation occurs within 3 business days of signing the enrollment agreement
  • Cancellation occurs before the first day of classes
  • The program is cancelled by the school

Withdrawal After Program Start

For students who withdraw after classes begin:

  • Week 1: 75% tuition refund
  • Week 2: 50% tuition refund
  • Week 3: 25% tuition refund
  • After Week 3: No tuition refund

Non-Refundable Fees

The following fees are non-refundable once paid:

  • Registration fees
  • Supply kit fees (if kit has been distributed)
  • Examination fees
  • Certification fees

Federally-Funded Programs

For WIOA and other federally-funded programs:

  • Students may withdraw at any time
  • No financial penalty to the student
  • Must follow federal reporting requirements
  • May affect future funding eligibility

Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed within 30 days of the withdrawal date. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund or withdraw from a program:

  1. Submit written notice to: refunds@elevateforhumanity.org
  2. Include: Student name, program name, reason for withdrawal
  3. Return any distributed materials or equipment
  4. Complete exit interview if required
