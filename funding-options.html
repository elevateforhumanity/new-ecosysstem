Equal Opportunity & Non-Discrimination

Elevate for Humanity is an equal opportunity employer and training provider. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or veteran status in our programs, services, activities, or employment practices.

Accommodations: We provide reasonable accommodations for individuals with disabilities. Contact our Accessibility Coordinator at (317) 555-0126 or accessibility@elevateforhumanity.org to request accommodations.

Language Services: Free language interpretation and translation services are available. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance to arrange services.

Complaints: If you believe you have experienced discrimination, contact our Equal Opportunity Officer at (317) 555-0127 or file a complaint with the Indiana Civil Rights Commission.

Compliance Note: This information is current as of September 2024 and is subject to change based on federal and state regulations. Funding availability depends on appropriations and may be limited. All eligibility requirements must be verified through official channels.

ETPL Status: Elevate for Humanity maintains active status on Indiana's Eligible Training Provider List (ETPL) under WIOA. Our programs meet federal performance standards for employment outcomes, credential attainment, and earnings gains.
