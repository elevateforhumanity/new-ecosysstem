Rise Forward Foundation

Philanthropic Leadership Team

Our visionary leadership team is dedicated to transforming lives through strategic philanthropy and workforce development. Rise Forward Foundation founded Elevate for Humanity to create sustainable pathways from poverty to prosperity through technology education and community empowerment.

Mission-Driven Approach

  • 🎯 Strategic philanthropy focused on measurable impact
  • 💡 Innovation in workforce development and AI/Data Science training
  • 🤝 Building partnerships that create lasting community change
  • 📈 Proven track record of transforming lives through education

Organizational Structure

Through Selfish Inc. DBA Rise Foundation, our leadership team oversees a comprehensive ecosystem of social impact organizations, including Elevate for Humanity and strategic healthcare partnerships, all unified under a mission of sustainable community transformation.

Umbrella Organization Structure
  • Selfish Inc. DBA Rise Foundation - Philanthropic umbrella & fiscal intermediary
