Indiana Workforce Training That Pays for Itself

Partner with Elevate for Humanity – state and federal workforce funding can cover tuition, wages, equipment, mentoring and retention for eligible hires. We streamline pathways so you focus on productivity while people build durable careers. Explore our comprehensive training programs or learn how employers benefit from our partnership approach.

Schedule a Team Call – 317‑760‑7908

Employer Programs & Incentives

  • WEX (Work Experience): 100% wages covered up to 480 hours. Learn about federal funding opportunities.
  • OJT (On‑the‑Job Training): 50–75% wage reimbursement for 400–1,040 hours (save up to ~$14K per hire). Access employer resources.
  • JRI: Tuition + wages + barrier removal for justice‑involved talent. Check eligibility requirements.
  • WRG + ETPL: State-funded tuition for high‑demand credentials. View available training programs.
  • Registered Apprenticeships (RAPIDS): Earn & learn with incentive stacking. Take our skills assessment.
