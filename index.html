Indiana Workforce Training That Pays for Itself

Partner with Elevate for Humanity – state and federal workforce funding can cover tuition, wages, equipment, mentoring and retention for eligible hires. We streamline pathways so you focus on productivity while people build durable careers.

Schedule a Team Call – 317‑760‑7908

Employer Programs & Incentives

  • WEX (Work Experience): 100% wages covered up to 480 hours.
  • OJT (On‑the‑Job Training): 50–75% wage reimbursement for 400–1,040 hours (save up to ~$14K per hire).
  • JRI: Tuition + wages + barrier removal for justice‑involved talent.
  • WRG + ETPL: State-funded tuition for high‑demand credentials.
  • Registered Apprenticeships (RAPIDS): Earn & learn with incentive stacking.
