📈 Assessment Criteria

Automatic Advancement Thresholds:

  • 85%+ Score: 🟢 Automatic advancement to second interview
  • 70-84% Score: 🟡 Conditional advancement (manager review required)
  • Below 70%: 🔴 Polite rejection with constructive feedback

What We Assess:

  • Technical Knowledge: Accuracy and depth of technical responses
  • Problem-Solving: Logical approach and creative thinking
  • Communication: Clarity, structure, and explanation ability
  • Practical Application: Real-world understanding and examples
