Indiana Workforce Training That Pays for Itself

Partner with Elevate for Humanity – Indiana funds cover tuition, wages, and apprenticeships so employers save money and workers build careers.

Schedule a Team Meeting – 317-760-7908

Employer Programs

  • WEX: 100% wages covered up to 480 hours.
  • OJT: 50–75% wage reimbursement for 400–1,040 hours (save up to ~$14k per hire).
  • JRI: Tuition + wages + support for justice-involved talent.
  • WRG + ETPL: State-funded tuition for high-demand credentials.
  • Apprenticeships (RAPIDS): Earn & learn; state incentives available.
