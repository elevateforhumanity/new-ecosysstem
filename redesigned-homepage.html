Common Questions

Do I need experience or a college degree?

No! We start from the beginning. If you have a high school diploma or GED and can follow instructions, you can succeed in our programs.

How long do programs take?

Most programs are 4-12 weeks. We offer flexible schedules including evenings and weekends to work around your life.

Will I really get a job?

85% of our graduates find employment within 6 months. We have partnerships with local employers and provide job placement assistance.

What if I can't afford the program?

Most students pay $0 thanks to WIOA, Workforce Ready Grants, and employer sponsorship. We'll help you find funding options.

Are classes online or in-person?

We offer hybrid options - some online learning combined with hands-on practice in our modern facilities.
