Our Mission

Rise Forward is dedicated to breaking barriers and creating pathways to success for individuals in underserved communities. Through comprehensive workforce development, educational programs, and community partnerships, we empower people to rise forward in their careers and lives.

Our Impact Areas

🎓

Skills Development

Comprehensive training programs designed to build essential job skills and professional competencies in high-demand industries.

🤝

Community Outreach

Direct engagement with underserved communities to identify needs, provide resources, and create lasting positive change.

💼

Job Readiness

Holistic preparation for employment including resume building, interview skills, workplace etiquette, and career planning.

💻

Digital Literacy

Essential computer and internet skills training to ensure participants can thrive in today's digital workplace.

🏢

Employer Partnerships

Building relationships with local employers to create direct pathways from our programs to sustainable employment.

📈

Career Advancement

Ongoing support and mentorship to help program graduates continue growing in their chosen career paths.

Our Partnership

Elevate for Humanity Partnership

Rise Forward operates as a nonprofit DBA (Doing Business As) under Elevate for Humanity, leveraging shared resources, expertise, and networks to maximize our community impact. This partnership allows us to maintain our nonprofit mission while benefiting from established infrastructure and proven methodologies in workforce development.
