Our Mission

Rise Forward is dedicated to breaking barriers and creating pathways to success for individuals in underserved communities. Through comprehensive workforce development, educational programs, and community partnerships, we empower people to rise forward in their careers and lives.

Our Partnership

Elevate for Humanity Partnership

Rise Forward operates as a nonprofit DBA (Doing Business As) under Elevate for Humanity, leveraging shared resources, expertise, and networks to maximize our community impact. This partnership allows us to maintain our nonprofit mission while benefiting from established infrastructure and proven methodologies in workforce development.
