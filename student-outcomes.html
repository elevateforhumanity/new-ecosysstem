Data Collection & Methodology

Data Sources

  • Employment Data: Indiana Department of Workforce Development wage records
  • Earnings Data: State wage records supplemented by graduate surveys
  • Credential Data: Direct tracking of certifications and program completions
  • Completion Data: Internal student information system records

Reporting Period

  • Program Year: July 1, 2023 - June 30, 2024
  • Follow-up Periods: Q2 (4-6 months post-exit), Q4 (10-12 months post-exit)
  • Data Collection: Ongoing through state wage record matching

Privacy & Confidentiality

  • All individual student data is protected and anonymized
  • Performance data aggregated to prevent identification
  • Compliance with FERPA and state privacy requirements
  • Data sharing agreements with state agencies for wage record matching
