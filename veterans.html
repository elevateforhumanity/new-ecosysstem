What is Priority of Service?

Priority of service means that covered persons receive priority over non-covered persons for the receipt of employment, training, and placement services provided under qualified job training programs.

This priority applies to all services funded in whole or in part by the U.S. Department of Labor, ensuring veterans and eligible spouses get first access to workforce development opportunities.

Federal Requirement

Priority of service is mandated by 20 CFR 1010 and applies to all DOL-funded workforce development programs.
